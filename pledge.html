ENVIRONMENTAL PLEDGE

Curing the modern throwaway culture.

At Donkey Jaw Services, we recognize that deferred maintenance is a massive contributor to environmental damage. When people fail to maintain their vehicles, HVAC systems, and heavy equipment, it directly leads to premature landfill waste, toxic fluid leaks, and skyrocketing carbon emissions from inefficient engines.

Our flagship app, Maintenance Vault, is built to address a deeply personal and globally critical problem: the paralyzing anxiety of climate change combined with this everyday deferred maintenance. We want to give everyday people a tangible, accessible way to fight climate anxiety by extending the lifecycle of their property and reducing their personal carbon footprint.

The app acts as a comprehensive digital tracking system that goes beyond simple calendar reminders by integrating an "environmental rating" for our users. For example, changing a vehicle or HVAC filter on time isn't just a chore—it optimizes energy consumption and reduces emissions.

We are converting the climate anxiety that so many of us feel into localized, measurable action. By empowering our users to properly maintain their assets, we are building a community that actively prevents premature equipment disposal and lowers emissions, one routine check at a time.
